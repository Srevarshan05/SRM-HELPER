FOSS IEEE Chapter of SRM IST Trichy

We are the Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) IEEE Student Chapter at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Trichy. Our mission is to promote open-source development, foster innovation, and create impactful solutions for the student community. SRM Verse is one of our flagship projects, designed to streamline academic resources and enhance the learning experience for all students.
